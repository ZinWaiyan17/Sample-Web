Introduction

The Vintage Rally Club of New South Wales is the home of classic navigational rallying on the east coast of Australia. The Vally will take place across five contries. They are Australia, Brazil, Canada, France and Netherlands. Vintage rallying involves recreational car rally events held on sealed roads open to the public. The emphasis is on using navigational skills to follow route instructions.

Any vehicle that is road registered and has at least two seats is eligible to enter events.

On our website you can see all about classic rallying, see what rallies are drives and a co-driver who assists the driver. Co-drivers play an important role such as navigating or time management in a competition, so that drivers can concentrate on driving. You can register online and Contact us to participate our Vintage Rally Club.

We welcome people who are interested to come along to our monthly meetings.
